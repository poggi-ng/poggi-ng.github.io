Alex Nunn

Alexander “Alex” Nunn is a computer science major at California Polytechnic State University from Scottsdale, Arizona. He is a professional Minecraft PVPer, Pokémon strategist, golfer, and content producer for his YouTube and Twitch channels. He is most well-known for doing the "pog" face, a popular meme in Internet culture. He is also known for his catch-phrase in Minecraft PvP, “I'm on the bard!”
